## How to Use

### Open in Cytoscape
1. Download and install Cytoscape from https://cytoscape.org
2. Open `network/regulatory_network.graphml`
3. Apply layout: Layout → Attribute Circle Layout
4. Color nodes by type (TF vs Target)

### Interactive Visualization
1. Open `figures/interactive_network.html` in your browser
2. Hover over nodes and edges for details
3. Zoom and pan to explore the network
